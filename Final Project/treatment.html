How to Find Treatment:

There are several ways in NYC to find a health care provider who treats opioid addiction with buprenorphine or find a methadone treatment program. You can:

  • Search a list of methadone treatment providersin New York and across the U.S.
  • Search map of buprenorphine and other treatment providers in and around the city.
  • Call (888) NYC-Well (888-692-9355), or text “WELL” to 651-73. NYC Well is the city’s free, confidential 24/7 hotline available to all New Yorkers seeking support for drug use and other problems.
  • Talk to your doctor.
